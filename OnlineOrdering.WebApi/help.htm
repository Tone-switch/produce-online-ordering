How To Use This Site

Viewing Products

Use the category listing on the left menu to view products by category groups. Products are displayed alphabetically within each category. Certain products may not be available for a particular category depending upon your login status, availability and price. To view a master alphabetical list click on the  Master List icon.

Adding Products to Your Order

To add products to your order find the the desired product either by category or by the master list. Select the unit type that you want to order. Each product displayed may have up to three unit types (case, each or lb or a derivitive thereof). For example to purchase a case of Apples select (click on ) the "Cs." unit value (see below). This will add one case unit to your order cart. If an item is not sold by a particular unit, that unit will not be displayed. You could also order this product by the pound or each by clicking on either the "Ea." or "Lb." units.

APP100 Apples, Red Delicious 100 ct 100 / 38 $38.00 Cs. $.18 Ea. $.45 Lb.

Viewing & Editing Your Order Cart

You may view your order cart at any time by clicking on the My Order icon. You may chage the quantity of any item in the cart by changing the quantity value for the item and clicking on the "Update " button (see below).

        

There are several other actions available when viewing you order cart. You may "Save" your cart and reload (Load Saved) it as you default order cart at any time you login to this site. Comments about the items you order may also be entered by using the "Add Comments" button.

Submitting Your Order

To submit your order click on the "Submit" button. On the confirmation page please select the desired delvery date and enter any other required information. Note: You may be required to enter delivery address information if your account information is not available in our system.

After all of the required information is complete click on the button to place tour order on our server. A confirmation message will be displayed that may be printed for your records.
